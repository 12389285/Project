Terrorism

Background of terroristic incidents

The main definition of terrorism is: The use of violence or threat of violence in the pursuit of political, religious, ideological or social objectives. Also it can be committed by a goverment, non-state actors or undercover personel serving on the behalf of their respective goverments. Mostly the purpose of an attack is the target of a large number of victems. Where the main purpose is to create a terror amoung masses of people, mostly innocent. The first time the term "terrorist" was used was in 1794 by the French philosopjer Franscois-Noel Babeuf. Over the years there are over 109 differnt definitions of terrorim.

Visualization of the attacks

The purpose of using this website is to see how the attacks of terrorits developed over the years. The start of this datasheet started in 1970 and ends in 2017. When clicking on the page terrorism there will first be a visualization of a worldmap to see the evolution of the amount of yearly attacks in the world.

Barchart

In the barchart on the right of the worldmap you can select countries in the worldmap to compare yearly attacks per country or of course per year. Using the slider it is possible to switch between the year 1970 up to 2017.

Linechart

With all the attacks there are a lot of innocent people who died or got seriously injured by one of the attacks. When selecting a country in the worldmap the linechart will automatically change to that specific country. The linechart will show two lines where the red line stands for the number of deaths during that year. The blue line shows the number of people who got injured that year. See also for the world deaths and injured people by clicking on the buttons above the linechart. In the dorpdown all the countries in the world are listed in a alphabetical order.
